SocPlay Design System

A broadcast-grade live-scores system. Energetic, premium, fast — a modern sports overlay crossed with a clean data product. Tokens map 1:1 to a Vue + Tailwind build.

01 — Brand

Personality & logo

Confident, alive, scoreboard-first. The volt accent signals “live energy” and primary action; everything else stays calm and high-contrast so the scores do the shouting.

08 — Accessibility

Built in, not bolted on

✓WCAG AA contrastText and accents meet AA in both themes; the light-theme accent darkens to hold contrast on white.
✓Visible focus & keyboard nav2px accent focus ring on every interactive element; cards and nav are Enter-activatable.
✓ARIA live regionGoals announce to screen readers: “Goal for France, Mbappé, 74 minutes. Score now 2–1.”
✓Color-blind-safe statusWin/draw/loss and live use distinct shapes & icons in addition to color.
✓Reduced motionAll animation collapses under prefers-reduced-motion and the Settings toggle.
